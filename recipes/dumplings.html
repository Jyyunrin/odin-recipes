Dumplings

Description

A staple in asian cuisine, dumplings are simple to make, delicious, and filling for all members of the family after a long day's of work.

Ingredients

  • Dumpling wrapper
  • Flour
  • Ground Pork
  • Chives
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut up the chives and mix with the ground pork
  2. Scoop enough filling of the chive-pork mixture into a wrapper and close it with water and flour
  3. Boil dumplings in water for 10 minutes in high heat
  4. Serve with soy sauce

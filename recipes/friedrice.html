Fried Rice

Description

This describes the recipe of fried rice, delicious with shrimp, bbq pork, and more. Savour the taste of asia with this mouth-watering, tasty recipe!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Shrimp
  • Bbq Pork
  • Egg
  • Various vegetables
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. First, cook your rice
  2. Fry the egg and remove it from the pan
  3. Add oil and cook your shrimp, pork, and vegetables
  4. Add the rice once everything is cooked
  5. Add the egg back in
  6. Add the sesame oil and any other seasoning you prefer

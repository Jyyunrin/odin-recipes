Soup Noodles

Description

Savour the delicious broth of chinese soup noodles, easy to make and ready in minutes!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Scallions
  • Chicken broth
  • Any kind of protein and vegetable

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles in water until soft
  2. Take them out and rinse in cold water
  3. Pan fry the vegetables and proteins (proteins first)
  4. Throw them into half a mixture of water and chicken broth
  5. Add the noodles in
  6. Top it off with scallions
